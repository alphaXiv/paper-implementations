## ABOUT THE AUTHOR 

Kevin Kurtz is the author of the nonfiction children's books A Day in the Deep, A Day on the Mountain, and A Day in the Salt Marsh, as well as Uncovering Earth's Secrets, which was also illustrated by Alice Feagan. He was onboard the JOIDES Resolution during a research expedition in which microbiologist Jason Sylvan looked for microbes living in extinct, underwater volcanoes. To learn more about Kevin's books and school visit programs, visit http://kevinkurtz.homestead.com.
